About us

Our story

Where do we make appointments the most often? Where do we hurry in the morning to drink aromatic coffee, have breakfast or eat a cake or croissant before work? Where do we go in the evening to skip the rush hour, drink a glass of wine or a cocktail, calmly make plans for the next day or just scroll through the Facebook feed?

To the coffee shop. It's cozy here. It's delicious here. Here, from the first visit, the waiters will remember your favorite coffee, and from the third they will ask: "Should I bring our regular order?"

Having started in 1972 as a small family cafe, Waff&Love is still a place where you feel just like at home.

What we do

Great coffee
Our visitors can taste different sorts of coffee from many countries. Our collectoin includes 20+ types of coffee and 30+ types of tea.

Incredible desserts
We cook all desserts in our cafe using only natural products. This is the key to the quality of our sweets that are popular among guests.

Musical evenings
On some Fridays, you can hear bright covers of famous tunes performed by musicians.
